Phil's Players

A set of video players with simple GUIs for video engineers testing purposes.

  • VideoJS with Contrib HLS
  • VideoJS with Contrib Dash (With Widevine) and Chromecast
  • Shaka Player (DASH)
  • DashJS (DashIF Reference Player)
  • Brightcove Player (Dash CENC with Silverlight fallback)
  • Brightcove Player (HLS)
